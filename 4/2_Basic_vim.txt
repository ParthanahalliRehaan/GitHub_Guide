---

## 📝 Getting Started
- **Open a file:** `vim filename`
- **Interactive tutorial:** `vimtutor`
- **Help system:** `:help`

---

## 🔄 Modes in Vim
- **Normal mode (default):** Navigation & commands → press `<Esc>`
- **Insert mode:** Typing text → `i` (before cursor), `a` (after cursor), `o` (new line below), `O` (new line above)
- **Visual mode:** Select text → `v` (character), `V` (line), `<C-v>` (block)
- **Command mode:** Execute commands → `:` at bottom
- **Replace mode:** Overwrite text → `R`

---

## 🎯 Navigation
- **Character movement:** `h` (left), `l` (right)
- **Line movement:** `0` (line start), `^` (first non-blank), `$` (line end)
- **Word movement:** `w` (next word), `b` (previous word), `e` (end of word)
- **Screen movement:** `H` (top), `M` (middle), `L` (bottom), `<C-f>` (page down), `<C-b>` (page up)
- **File movement:** `gg` (top of file), `G` (bottom), `nG` (go to line n)

---

## ✂️ Editing
- **Delete:** `x` (char), `dd` (line), `dw` (word), `D` (to end of line)
- **Change:** `cw` (change word), `cc` (change line), `C` (to end of line)
- **Replace:** `r` (single char), `R` (replace mode)
- **Copy & Paste:** `yy` (copy line), `yw` (copy word), `p` (paste after), `P` (paste before)

---

## 💾 Saving & Quitting
- **Save:** `:w`
- **Save & quit:** `:wq`
- **Quit:** `:q`
- **Quit without saving:** `:q!`

---

## 🔍 Search & Replace
- **Search:** `/pattern` → `n` (next), `N` (previous)
- **Replace:** `:%s/old/new/g` (replace all in file)

---

## 📑 Useful Extras
- **Undo/Redo:** `u` (undo), `<C-r>` (redo)
- **Line numbers:** `:set number` (show), `:set nonumber` (hide)
- **Syntax highlighting:** `:syntax on`

---
